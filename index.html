    About Me

    Hi, I'm Yeganeh! I'm a beginner researcher, collaborating with H2Lab at the University of Washington and JHU Center for Language and Speech Processing, and I have the privilege to be mentored by Daniel Khashabi and Yizhong Wang. Also, I'm a teaching assistant at Tehran Polytechnic.

    I graduated from Tehran Polytechnic in 2021 with a double bachelor's degree in Electrical Engineering and Computer Science (Known as Computer Engineering in Iran).

    I'm mainly interested in AI, ML, and NLP. My current research is focused on teaching machines via instructions, improving the performance of Language Models in zero-shot setting, and moving toward generalization and unification.

    If you have any questions about my work or research/academia/programming-related topics, please feel free to reach out!
